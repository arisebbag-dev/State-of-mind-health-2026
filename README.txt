PLACE YOUR IMAGES HERE
======================

The site references these images. Add them to this /public folder:

1. nechama.jpg
   - A warm, professional headshot of Nechama Keller-Sabel.
   - Used in the "Meet Nechama" section on the homepage.
   - Recommended: portrait orientation, at least 800 x 1000px.
   - To use it, open /components/ProviderTeaser.jsx and replace the
     placeholder block with Next.js <Image src="/nechama.jpg" ... />.

2. og-image.jpg (optional but recommended)
   - A 1200 x 630px social-share image (logo + tagline on brand color).
   - Reference it in app/layout.jsx under openGraph.images.

3. favicon.ico
   - Drop a favicon.ico into the /app folder (Next.js picks it up automatically),
     or add icon.png / apple-icon.png to /app for auto-generated icons.
